## Long (≈3500 chars) — full App Store description

**GearSnitch — the command center for everything you train with.**

If you've ever stared at four open apps just to finish one workout, GearSnitch is for you. We built one place for every piece of gear, every heart rate reading, every set, every supplement — and we made it respect your privacy while doing it.

**Pair any Bluetooth gear in seconds**
Heart straps, smart dumbbells, treadmills, rowers, blood pressure cuffs — if it speaks BLE, GearSnitch finds it. No menu diving. No reconnect dance. Your gear shows up and stays connected.

**Live split heart rate**
Watch two heart rate sources side by side. Compare your chest strap against your Watch. See training load in real time. Know when to push and when to back off.

**Full workout + run tracking**
Log strength sessions with PRs and rest timers. Start a run and watch your route draw itself on the map. Heart rate zones, pace splits, and auto-lap are all standard.

**Peptide and supplement journal**
A private, on-device log for the things you take and the labs you run. Set dose reminders. Export to PDF when you see your doctor. This is not a medical device and does not diagnose — it's your journal, not our opinion.

**Apple Watch companion**
Start workouts from your wrist. Glance at live HR. Control music. Leave your phone in the locker.

**Gym geofence check-in**
Walk in. Your session starts. Walk out. Your session ends. No tapping buttons.

**Referrals and rewards**
Invite a friend. They get a discount, you get store credit. Share via QR code or link.

**Built-in store**
Branded apparel, straps, and gear, fulfilled direct.

**Privacy-first by design**
- Health data is encrypted and stored under your account.
- No third-party analytics trackers.
- No ad networks.
- You can export or delete everything from Settings.
- We do not sell your data. Not now, not ever.

**Subscription details**
GearSnitch Pro unlocks unlimited gear slots, advanced analytics, PDF exports, and priority support. Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the period. Manage or cancel anytime in your Apple ID settings. Prices and plans are shown in-app before you confirm.

Train smart. Connect everything. Keep it private.
