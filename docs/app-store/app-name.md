# App Store Name Candidates

Per S1 brand decision. Founder to pick one. Unused candidates can be held as subtitles or alt marketing names.

## Rules
- **App name:** 30 characters max (App Store Connect hard limit).
- **Subtitle:** 30 characters max.
- **Promotional text:** 170 chars (editable post-submission without review).
- Avoid trademarked terms (e.g., "Apple Watch", "Bluetooth" — use "BLE"/"wireless").
- Do not use "for" + brand name; Apple rejects.
